On May 11, 2026, I spoke at the **LSEG Quant Summit London 2026** held in Paternoster Square, London. The event focused on the latest developments in the Open-Source Risk Engine (ORE) ecosystem.

My presentation, **"ORE in the Era of Agentic AI"**, explored how Large Language Models (LLMs) and AI agents are transforming quantitative finance. Key topics covered in the talk included:

*   The capabilities and limitations of modern LLMs in writing pricing and risk models.
*   Real-world use cases of AI agents automating workflows and creating value within the ORE framework.
*   The integration of the Model Context Protocol (MCP) to connect AI agents directly with specialized quantitative tools.
*   LSEG's collaborative efforts in this space, including partnerships with leading AI labs like Anthropic.

It was fantastic to connect with other quantitative researchers, developers, and risk professionals to discuss the future of open-source risk analytics and artificial intelligence.
